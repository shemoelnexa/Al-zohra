Villas for Sale in Ajman: The Complete Buyer's Guide to Al Zorah

Al Zorah Sales Team
28 June 2026 8 min read

Why Ajman Is Becoming a Villa Buyer's Destination

Villa buyers priced out of Dubai's most established communities are increasingly looking north. Ajman, once seen mainly as the affordable alternative, has become a serious destination in its own right for anyone who wants to buy a villa in Ajman without compromising on setting, and Al Zorah sits at the centre of that shift. Built around an eighteen-hole championship golf course, a protected mangrove reserve and kilometres of coastline, it offers a style of villa living that is hard to find elsewhere in the northern Emirates.

This guide covers what you need to know before you buy: the villa types on offer, indicative pricing by development, freehold ownership and Golden Visa eligibility, available payment plans, and how to book a viewing.

Villa Types Available at Al Zorah

Al Zorah's villa portfolio spans two distinct tiers, each built around a different piece of the destination's geography.

Sea Glints Mansions: oceanfront, premium tier

Sea Glints Mansions sit directly on the waterfront and are the top of the portfolio: five-bedroom villas on plots of around 12,900 square feet, with built-up living space of roughly 7,300 square feet. Positioned as exclusive oceanfront mansions, this is the largest footprint available at Al Zorah, and it ranks among the most distinctive luxury villas UAE buyers can find outside Dubai.

Beach Hills Villas: elevated coastal living

Beach Hills Villas occupy a raised coastal position, available as both simplex and duplex layouts. Four-bedroom simplex units sit on plots of around 12,400 square feet, while duplex villas start from roughly 7,400 square feet. Both configurations are built around sea views and private beach access, at a more accessible entry point than Sea Glints Mansions.

Freehold Ownership and Golden Visa Eligibility for Villa Buyers

Al Zorah sits inside a designated freehold zone, meaning villa ownership is open to UAE nationals and foreign buyers alike, with full title to both the unit and the land it sits on. This is one of the reasons the destination has drawn international buyers rather than only domestic ones.

Villa buyers at Al Zorah may also be eligible for the UAE's Golden Visa. The programme grants a renewable ten-year residence permit to property owners whose UAE real estate is valued at AED 2 million or more. Given that even entry-level three-bedroom villas at Al Zorah are valued above that figure, most of the portfolio would meet the valuation threshold on its own, including Beach Hills Villas and Sea Glints Mansions.

Eligibility turns on the property's total value, not simply the amount paid to date, and the exact documentation required can differ between ready, off-plan and mortgaged property. A booking deposit alone should never be assumed to secure eligibility. Confirm your specific position with a conveyancer or directly with the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security before relying on Golden Visa eligibility as part of any purchase decision, since criteria are updated periodically. Payment Plans and Post-Handover Options

Payment structures at Al Zorah vary by development, but the general shape is consistent with how off-plan villas are typically sold in the UAE: buyers pay a portion on booking, a further portion through construction, and the balance at or after handover.

Post-handover plans, where a share of the price is paid over one to three years following completion, tend to suit investors more than end-users, since they stretch financing without requiring occupancy to begin immediately. This structure also lets an investor rent the villa out and offset part of the ongoing payments against rental income. Buyers planning to live in the villa from day one more often lean toward front-loaded, construction-linked plans instead, since they clear the balance closer to the point they actually move in.

Because terms differ between Sea Glints Mansions and Beach Hills Villas, and can change as each phase sells down, request the current plan for your specific villa type directly from the sales team rather than relying on figures published elsewhere.
